2 Materials and Methods

The data was analyzed in the statistical software R (R Core Team 2019). The germination analysis and graphics was carried out with the package GerminaR (Blum 2005). Each variable was submitted at analysis of variance (ANOVA) and the mean comparison test used was Student-Newman Keuls (P<0.05)(Müller y Wickham 2019). For the multivariate analysis, the principal components analysis (PCA) and cluster hierarchical classification analysis (HCPC) will be used (Gutierrez et al. 2018).

The vertical bars represent the means (±SE). The mean differences between the groups are represented by different capital letters and into the group different lowercase letters (SNK, p = 0.05) (Gutierrez et al. 2018).
